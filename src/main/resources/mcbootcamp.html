MC Web Development Bootcamp

No Rest for the Weary

To address the shortage in the number of people with the skills to perform the growing number of information technology, data science and cybersecurity roles Montgomery College four years ago began offering an intensive eight-week web development bootcamp.

The class meets daily from 9 am. to 5 p.m. The course load is very demanding. Comprehending the concepts and completing the assignments requires a lot of staying power.

Due to the coronavirus outbreak the class has moved to a remote learning environment. Students and their instructors meet via a video conferencing service to deliver and hear lectures and complete group projects.

The curriculum seeks to pack in many topics. So each day instructors introduce a new concept. For example, in week three students were introduced to html coding and by the end of the week were challenged to produce a seven-page professional blog site.

Overall, I'm happy to be enrolled and hope that the intense training, compressed format, long class days, long nights and weekends will result in me learning these concepts and pursuing projects that help people share ideas and tell their stories.
